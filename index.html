My Identity

Welcome to my World

Together, we will dicover my identity through some traditional Afican Fabrics

Those Fabrics, really defined who we are and can actually tell about which ethnic you belong to toward their patterns

There are 3 different types of those fabrics that I want to talk about here

  • Kanvor, which is originally from Benin and Togo in WEST Africa. It is made of coton and wear for traditional event.
  • Kente, which is a traditional Ghanaian fabric made of slick of cotton, knows for its bright colors and symbolic patterns
  • Bogolanfini, which is a handmade fabric dyed with fermented mud

This is what defined my identity, through those fabrics, people can know my story and where I come from.
